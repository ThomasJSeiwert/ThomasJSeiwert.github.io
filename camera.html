Thomas J Seiwert

DogBox

2020 / Industrial Design




A Dog Feeder and Camera With a Cute Aesthetic

Dogbox is a dog feeder that contains a camera and food dispensor.

Control the dog feeder through your phone and keep track of your pet while you're away.

DogBox contains speakers as well so you can talk to your special buddy anytime.


Special Thanks

Special thanks to Proffessor Noah Wangerin for mentoring me.
