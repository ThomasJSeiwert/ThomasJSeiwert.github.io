DogBox


DogBox is a fun alternative to the pet feeder with a camera. There are a lot of similar products on the market, however DogBox is designed to be colorful, cheap and easy to use.

DogBox


DogBox is a fun alternative to the pet feeder with a camera. There are a lot of similar products on the market, however DogBox is designed to be colorful, cheap and easy to use.









Pour food easily into the back of the device to store food for your dog. Hold down the power button to turn on the device. Charge the device using the USB C port.


The inside of Dog Box contains two speakers representing the dog’s eyes, a camera representing its nose and a small conveyer belt for opening the door mechanism.


Use the Dog Box app for a variety of different functionalities as shown above.
