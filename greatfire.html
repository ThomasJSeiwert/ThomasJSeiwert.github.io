Great Fire Productions Logo Design


Film production company Great Fire Productions hired me as a Graphic Design Contractor to create a logo that would represent their brand. I designed various logos for them to choose from.





These logos were inspired by the Great Chicago Fire of 1987. the goal was to take a tragedy that brought people together and turn it into an iconic logo that represents the strength and perseverance of Chicago.
