Rice Cooker


Myself and a group of students were tasked to create a rice cooker for a specific demographic of people. We worked together as a team to develop market research, concept sketches and renderings. We decided on the final features of the design together.





Our chosen demographics for this project were older individuals and college students.









Our original concepts experimented with form and the function of the cooker. Each rice cooker had a heating dock meant to be detached from the cooker when needed.
