AED Emergency Station

2024 / Industrial Design




A Versatile Emergency Station

This emergency station contains an AED, speaker, extra

AED, fire extinguisher, help button and fire hydrant.


Stay safe and be prepared for multiple emergencies.

Call 911 to unlock AED


Press the help button and speak to a park security officer.

Fire fighters can use the hydrant to protect against fires.

Civilians can use a fure extinguisher to put out fires.
