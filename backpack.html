College Backpack

2020 / Industrial Design

A Backpack for College Students on the Go

This packpack is great for storing every college essential. There are two pockets for devices.

Stay energized during the day. Keep meals and snacks at the top of the backpack

The design is sleek and lightweight as well as sturdy enough to hold anything you need
