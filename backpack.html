Thomas J Seiwert

College Backpack

2020 / Industrial Design




A Backpack for College Students on the Go

This backpack is great for organizing every college essential. There are two compartments for devices on the side of the bag.

Stay energized during the day. Keep meals and snacks at the top of the backpack. add an icepack to keep food cool.

The design is sleek and lightweight as well as sturdy enough to hold anything you need


Special Thanks

Special thanks to Professor Noah Wangerin for mentoring me.
