Trash Module for Fiat Chrysler Automobiles


The Company of Fiat Chrysler Automobiles asked students at University of Illinois to collaborate with them and design solutions to current car models. My team was given the Chrysler Pacifica.



The problem as shown is that it is too common for car owners to leave trash in the car which builds up over time.
