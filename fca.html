FCA Trash Module

2018 / Industrial Design





❮ ❯

A convienent way to remove trash and keep your car clean.

The FCA Trash module uses a cartridge containing trash bags.

Simply fill the console with any trash. When your bag is full, rip out the bag.

When your cartridge runs out of bags, just replace the cartridge.
