AED Emergency Station


This emergency station contains an AED, speaker, extra AED, fire extinguisher, help button and fire hydrant. Stay safe and be prepared for multiple emergencies. Call 911 to unlock AED Press the help button and speak to a park security officer. Fire fighters can use the hydrant to protect against fires. Civilians can use a fire extinguisher to put out fires.

Above is the original concept containing the features for this design. Details such as the five doors for the main AED enclosure, the touch sensor that contacts the police and the overall aesthetic of the station were simplified to be more easily understood by someone in crisis.
