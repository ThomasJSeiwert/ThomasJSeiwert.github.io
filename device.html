HearRing Sound Gem

2020 / Industrial Design / Thesis




Home Alert Device for the Deaf

The HearRing Gem is a device that alerts the deaf of

sounds within their home.


Control the way you recieve alerts through customizable

options that are controled through the HearRing app.


The HearRing gem displays icons, flashes and vibrates

based on what you need for your home.
